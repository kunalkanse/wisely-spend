Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect information that you provide directly to us and information that is automatically collected when you use our service.

Information you provide:

  • Account information (email address, password)
  • Expense data (amounts, categories, descriptions, dates)
  • Profile information you choose to provide
  • Communications you send to us

Automatically collected information:

  • Device information (browser type, operating system)
  • Usage data (pages visited, features used, time spent)
  • Log data (IP address, access times, error logs)

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our service. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Create and manage your Spendly account
  • Store and organize your expense data
  • Generate spending reports and insights
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • Improve our service and develop new features
  • Send important service updates and notifications
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our platform

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and security practices. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Key security measures include:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure password storage using industry-standard hashing
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account information and expense data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. If you choose to delete your account, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain certain information for legal or regulatory purposes.

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us through our support channels.

5. Third Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services to help operate our platform. These services may have access to your information only to perform tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for other purposes.

Third-party services we may use include:

  • Cloud hosting and infrastructure providers
  • Analytics services (with anonymized data)
  • Email delivery services
  • Customer support tools

6. Your Rights

You have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can view and export your expense data through your account
  • Correction: You can update your account information and expense data at any time
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability: You can export your expense data in a standard format

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through our support channels. We will respond to your inquiries promptly and work to address any concerns you may have.

For privacy-related matters, you can also reach out to us directly, and we will ensure your inquiry is handled with appropriate priority and care.
